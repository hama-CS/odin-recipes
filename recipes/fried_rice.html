Fried Rice

Description

Fried Rice is an easy to make and flavorful dish that even a novice cook like me can make. Feel free to experiment outside of my recipe below to make the dish yours!

What you will need:

  • 3 cups of cooked white rice
  • 3 tbsps of sesame oil
  • 1 cup of frozen peas and carrots
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 tsp of garlic, minced
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce

Steps:

  1. Preheat a large skillet or wok to medium heat.
  2. sesame oil in the bottom.
  3. Add white rice, frozen peas and carrots, and chopped onion.
  4. Stir fry until tender.
  5. Slide the rice mixture to one side of the skillet and pour beaten eggs onto the other side.
  6. Using a spatula, scramble the eggs.
  7. Once cooked, mix the eggs with the rice mixture.
  8. Stir in soy sauce and mix well. Serve hot.
